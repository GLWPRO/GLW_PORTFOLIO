I am a dedicated professional with over a decade of experience spanning program leadership, quality assurance, residential management, and professional development instruction. My work is rooted in aligning care, compliance, and training with measurable outcomes and continuous improvement. Along the way, I’ve had the privilege of speaking on professional leadership panels with the National Leadership Consortium, where I encourage and mentor new cohorts to excel in their academics and prepare for impactful careers.

Outside of my professional achievements, I find balance and inspiration through my personal interests. I enjoy reading, cooking, drawing, and exploring digital design and creative writing—all of which fuel my creativity and problem-solving mindset in the workplace. I also curate digital resources such as eBooks and online courses, blending my passion for design with my commitment to education and growth.

On a personal level, one of my greatest joys is being a devoted aunt to eight amazing nieces and nephews. They keep my spirit young, my heart grateful, and remind me daily of the importance of patience, play, and perspective.

At the heart of my journey is a belief in servant leadership, adaptability, and the power of community. Whether I am leading a team, supporting individuals through challenges, or building innovative projects, my goal remains the same: to leave a lasting, positive impact.

I’m happy to meet you, and I look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and organizations who share a passion for growth, service, and excellence.
